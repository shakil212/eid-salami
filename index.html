Your Privacy is Our Promise 🔒

We take the confidentiality of your information seriously

🔐

Data Confidentiality

All personal information including your name, ID details, and mobile number are stored securely and never shared with any third party.

👁️

Anonymous Public Statistics

Only total application counts and amounts are shown publicly. Individual applications, names, and ID information are never displayed to other users.

🚫

No Data Selling

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to any outside parties under any circumstances whatsoever.

📱

Payment Privacy

This website does not process or store any payment information. All transactions happen directly in your mobile banking app.

🗑️

Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your submitted application and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

✅

Purpose Limitation

Your information is collected solely for the purpose of Eid Salami distribution and for no other purpose.

We are committed to protecting your personal information. All data submitted through this platform is handled with the highest level of care and security. We collect only the minimum information necessary to process Salami applications, and we maintain strict controls to ensure your data is never misused.
